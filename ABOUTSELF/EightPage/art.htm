Digital Arts

Making digital art of my friends or idols is awesome because it lets me show what's cool about them in a new way. When I create on a screen, it's like bringing their personality to life. Whether it's a friend's big laugh or an idol's style, every tap and color helps me show how special they are. It's not just about making something pretty; it's about saying, "Hey, you're amazing!" And when they see it whether they smile big or laugh it feels really good. Making digital art of them is like giving them a special picture that shows how awesome they are.
