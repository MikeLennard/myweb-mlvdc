Dream Job

My dream is to be both a programmer and a photographer. As a programmer, I want to create amazing things using technology, like crafting apps or games that people love. It's about solving puzzles and making cool stuff with code. And then, there's photography I want to capture moments, tell stories through pictures, and show the world from my perspective. Whether it's writing code or clicking the perfect shot, both let me express myself in different ways. My dream job is all about blending these passions, using technology to create and photography to share the beauty I see.
